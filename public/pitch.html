What Makes Rhivo Different

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Each business gets isolated config, data, and subdomain with automatic wildcard DNS & TLS.

Smart Slot Generation

Respects business hours, timezone, capacity limits, and advance booking rules automatically.

Transactional Booking

Reservation tokens with TTL → atomic commit flow prevents race conditions.

Flexible Auth

Owner/staff/customer separation. Guest bookings supported. JWT-based row-level security.

Full Customization

Brand colors, services, pricing, availability rules—all YAML-configurable per tenant.

Notification System

Email + SMS with delivery tracking, retries, and idempotency for reliability.

Built on Modern Stack
